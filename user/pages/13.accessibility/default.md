# Accessibility Statement

**Last Updated:** January 2024

The Ogle County Economic Development Corporation ("OCEDC") is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

## Our Commitment

We strive to make our website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards to the greatest extent possible.

## Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

### Keyboard Navigation

- All interactive elements can be accessed using a keyboard
- Clear focus indicators for keyboard navigation
- Logical tab order throughout the site

### Screen Reader Compatibility

- Semantic HTML structure
- Alternative text for images
- Descriptive link text
- Proper heading hierarchy

### Visual Accessibility

- Sufficient color contrast ratios
- Text resizing capabilities
- Clear and readable fonts
- Consistent navigation structure

### Forms and Interactive Elements

- Clear form labels and instructions
- Error messages and validation feedback
- Accessible form controls

## Ongoing Efforts

We are continuously working to improve the accessibility of our website. Our efforts include:

- Regular accessibility audits
- User testing with assistive technologies
- Staff training on accessibility best practices
- Implementation of accessibility improvements
- Monitoring and addressing accessibility issues

## Known Limitations

While we strive to ensure accessibility, there may be some limitations:

- **Third-Party Content** - Some third-party content or widgets may not be fully accessible
- **Older Content** - Some older content may not meet current accessibility standards
- **PDF Documents** - Some PDF documents may not be fully accessible (we're working to improve this)

We are working to address these limitations and improve accessibility across all areas of our website.

## Alternative Access

If you need assistance accessing information on our website, we can provide:

- Alternative formats for content
- Assistance over the phone
- In-person assistance at our office

Please contact us to arrange alternative access to information.

## Standards and Guidelines

Our website aims to conform to:

- **WCAG 2.1 Level AA** - Web Content Accessibility Guidelines
- **Section 508** - Federal accessibility standards (where applicable)
- **ADA** - Americans with Disabilities Act requirements

## Feedback

We welcome feedback on how we can improve accessibility. Your input helps us identify and address accessibility barriers.

## Updates

We will update this Accessibility Statement as we make improvements to our website's accessibility.
