# Why Ogle County?

Ogle County offers a unique combination of strategic advantages that make it an ideal location for businesses of all sizes. From our prime location to our skilled workforce, discover what sets us apart.

## Strategic Location

### Prime Transportation Access

Ogle County sits at the crossroads of major transportation routes:

- **Interstate 39** - Direct north-south corridor connecting to I-80 and I-88
- **US Route 20** - Major east-west highway
- **Union Pacific Railroad** - Direct rail access for freight
- **Chicago Rockford International Airport** - Just 30 minutes away
- **O'Hare International Airport** - 90 minutes via I-39

### Proximity to Major Markets

- **Chicago** - 2 hours
- **Milwaukee** - 2 hours  
- **Rockford** - 30 minutes
- **Madison** - 2.5 hours
- **Quad Cities** - 1.5 hours

This strategic positioning gives businesses access to millions of consumers and a vast supply chain network.

## Tax Incentives & Financing

### Illinois EDGE Tax Credit

The Economic Development for a Growing Economy (EDGE) program provides tax credits to businesses that create jobs and make capital investments.

### Enterprise Zone Benefits

Properties in designated Enterprise Zones qualify for:
- Sales tax exemptions on building materials
- Investment tax credits
- Utility tax exemptions
- Property tax abatements

### TIF Districts

Tax Increment Financing districts provide public infrastructure improvements to support private development.

### Additional Programs

- SBA loan programs
- Illinois Finance Authority programs
- Local property tax abatements
- Workforce training grants
