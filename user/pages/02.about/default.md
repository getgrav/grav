# About Ogle County Economic Development Corporation

The **Ogle County Economic Development Corporation (OCEDC)** is dedicated to fostering economic growth, business development, and job creation throughout Ogle County, Illinois. We serve as the primary resource for businesses looking to expand, relocate, or start operations in our region.

## Our Mission

To promote and facilitate economic development in Ogle County by attracting new businesses, supporting existing businesses, and creating an environment that encourages investment, job growth, and community prosperity.

## Our Vision

To position Ogle County as the premier business destination in Northern Illinois, recognized for our strategic location, skilled workforce, business-friendly environment, and exceptional quality of life.

## Service Area

OCEDC serves all of Ogle County, including the communities of:

- **Oregon** - County seat and cultural hub
- **Rochelle** - Major transportation and logistics center
- **Byron** - Growing residential and commercial community
- **Polo** - Historic downtown and business district
- **Mount Morris** - Educational and residential center

## What We Do

### Business Attraction

We actively recruit businesses that align with our community values and economic goals. Our team provides comprehensive site selection assistance, market data, and connections to key stakeholders.

### Business Retention & Expansion

Supporting existing businesses is a top priority. We offer expansion planning, workforce development resources, and assistance navigating local regulations and incentives.

### Site Development

We maintain an inventory of available industrial and commercial sites, work with property owners to prepare sites for development, and facilitate connections between buyers and sellers.

### Workforce Development

Connecting businesses with skilled workers through partnerships with educational institutions, training programs, and workforce development initiatives.

### Community Advocacy

We advocate for policies and infrastructure improvements that support business growth and economic development throughout Ogle County.

## Leadership Team

Our dedicated team of professionals brings extensive experience in economic development, business assistance, and community relations.

### Board of Directors

OCEDC is governed by a volunteer board of directors representing diverse sectors of the Ogle County economy:

- **Business Leaders** - Representatives from manufacturing, retail, and service industries
- **Community Representatives** - Local government and community organization leaders
- **Financial Institutions** - Banking and financial services representatives
- **Educational Institutions** - Representatives from schools and training organizations
- **At-Large Members** - Community members committed to economic development

The board provides strategic direction, oversight, and governance for OCEDC's programs and initiatives.

### Staff

Our professional staff works directly with businesses to provide:

- Site selection assistance
- Business retention and expansion support
- Workforce development connections
- Incentive identification and application assistance
- Resource connections and referrals

### Committees

OCEDC operates through several committees that focus on specific areas:

- **Executive Committee** - Leadership and strategic planning
- **Marketing Committee** - Marketing and outreach initiatives
- **Site Development Committee** - Site selection and development
- **Events Committee** - Event planning and execution
