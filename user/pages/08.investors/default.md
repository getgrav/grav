# Become an OCEDC Investor

Join a network of forward-thinking businesses, organizations, and individuals who are investing in Ogle County's economic future. Your investment directly supports our mission to attract, retain, and grow businesses throughout the region.

## Why Become an Investor?

As an OCEDC investor, you're part of a collaborative effort to:

- **Attract New Businesses** - Support recruitment efforts that bring jobs and investment to Ogle County
- **Retain Existing Businesses** - Help local companies grow and expand
- **Develop Sites** - Contribute to site preparation and infrastructure development
- **Build Workforce** - Support training and workforce development programs
- **Advocate for Growth** - Amplify our voice in promoting economic development policies

## Investment Levels

We offer flexible investment levels to accommodate businesses and organizations of all sizes:

### Platinum Investor
**$5,000+ annually**

- Premier recognition at all OCEDC events
- Featured placement in investor directory
- Exclusive invitations to investor-only events
- Direct access to OCEDC leadership
- Priority site selection assistance
- Annual investor appreciation event

### Gold Investor
**$2,500 - $4,999 annually**

- Prominent recognition at OCEDC events
- Listing in investor directory
- Invitations to special events
- Regular updates on economic development activities
- Site selection assistance

### Silver Investor
**$1,000 - $2,499 annually**

- Recognition at OCEDC events
- Listing in investor directory
- Event invitations
- Economic development updates
- Business assistance services

### Bronze Investor
**$500 - $999 annually**

- Listing in investor directory
- Event invitations
- Economic development updates
- Business assistance services

### Individual Investor
**$100 - $499 annually**

- Listing in investor directory (optional)
- Event invitations
- Economic development updates

## Investment Process

Becoming an investor is simple:

1. **Review Investment Levels** - Choose the level that fits your organization
2. **Contact OCEDC** - Reach out to discuss your investment
3. **Complete Investment Form** - Provide basic information about your organization
4. **Make Payment** - Submit your annual investment
5. **Receive Benefits** - Start enjoying investor benefits immediately

## Annual Investment

Investments are made on an annual basis and can be paid:

- **Annually** - Single payment for the full year
- **Semi-Annually** - Two payments per year
- **Quarterly** - Four payments per year

## Renewal

Investors receive renewal notices 60 days before their investment anniversary. Renewing investors maintain continuous recognition and benefits.
