## Site Features

### Infrastructure

- **Transportation** - Direct access to I-39 and US Route 20
- **Rail** - Union Pacific rail access available
- **Utilities** - Water, sewer, natural gas, electric, fiber optic
- **Zoning** - Pre-zoned for industrial/commercial use
- **Environmental** - Phase I assessments completed

### Location Benefits

- **Enterprise Zones** - Tax incentives available
- **TIF Districts** - Public infrastructure support
- **Workforce** - Access to skilled labor pool
- **Markets** - 2 hours from Chicago, Milwaukee, Rockford
