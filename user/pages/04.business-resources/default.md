# Business Resources

Welcome to Ogle County's comprehensive business resource center. Whether you're starting a new business, expanding an existing operation, or exploring opportunities, we're here to help.

## How We Can Help

The Ogle County Economic Development Corporation provides free, confidential assistance to businesses at every stage:

- **Pre-Location** - Site selection, market analysis, incentive identification
- **Startup** - Permitting, licensing, workforce connections
- **Expansion** - Growth planning, financing, site development
- **Retention** - Problem-solving, resource connections, advocacy
