# Support Ogle County Economic Development Corporation

Your support helps drive economic growth, create jobs, and build a stronger future for Ogle County. There are multiple ways to get involved and make a difference.

## Why Support OCEDC?

The Ogle County Economic Development Corporation works tirelessly to:

- **Attract New Businesses** - Recruit companies that create jobs and invest in our community
- **Retain Existing Businesses** - Support local businesses in their growth and expansion
- **Develop Sites** - Prepare industrial and commercial properties for development
- **Build Workforce** - Connect businesses with skilled workers and training resources
- **Advocate for Growth** - Promote policies and infrastructure that support economic development

Your investment directly supports these efforts and helps create opportunities for all residents of Ogle County.

## Impact of Your Support

### Recent Achievements

Thanks to the support of our investors and community partners, OCEDC has:

- **Attracted New Businesses** - Multiple companies have located or expanded in Ogle County
- **Created Jobs** - Hundreds of new employment opportunities for residents
- **Developed Sites** - Prepared industrial and commercial properties for development
- **Supported Growth** - Assisted dozens of businesses with expansion and retention efforts
- **Built Partnerships** - Connected businesses with resources and stakeholders

### Community Benefits

When you support OCEDC, you're investing in:

- **Job Creation** - More employment opportunities for Ogle County residents
- **Economic Growth** - Increased business activity and investment
- **Tax Base Expansion** - More revenue for schools, infrastructure, and services
- **Quality of Life** - Stronger communities with more amenities and opportunities
- **Future Prosperity** - Sustainable economic development for generations to come
