# Volunteer with Ogle County Economic Development Corporation

Share your time, expertise, and passion for economic development by volunteering with OCEDC. Your involvement helps us achieve more and reach more businesses and communities.

## Why Volunteer?

Volunteering with OCEDC allows you to:

- **Make a Difference** - Contribute directly to economic growth and job creation
- **Build Your Network** - Connect with business leaders and community members
- **Develop Skills** - Gain experience in economic development and business assistance
- **Give Back** - Support your community and help build a stronger future
- **Stay Engaged** - Be part of exciting economic development initiatives

## Volunteer Opportunities

### Board & Committee Service

Serve on OCEDC's board of directors or committees:

- **Board of Directors** - Strategic leadership and governance
- **Executive Committee** - Leadership and oversight
- **Marketing Committee** - Marketing and outreach initiatives
- **Site Development Committee** - Site selection and development
- **Events Committee** - Planning and execution of events

**Time Commitment:** Monthly meetings plus occasional special projects

### Business Mentoring

Share your business expertise by mentoring:

- **New Business Owners** - Help startups navigate challenges
- **Expanding Businesses** - Provide guidance on growth strategies
- **Business Planning** - Assist with business plan development
- **Problem Solving** - Help businesses overcome obstacles

**Time Commitment:** Flexible, based on your availability

### Event Support

Help with OCEDC events and activities:

- **Investor Appreciation Events** - Planning and execution
- **Business Networking Events** - Coordination and support
- **Site Tours** - Hosting and logistics
- **Workshops & Seminars** - Planning and facilitation

**Time Commitment:** Varies by event, typically 2-4 hours per event

### Marketing & Outreach

Support marketing and communication efforts:

- **Content Creation** - Write articles, blog posts, or social media content
- **Photography** - Capture events and activities
- **Design** - Create marketing materials and graphics
- **Social Media** - Manage social media accounts

**Time Commitment:** Flexible, can work remotely

### Special Projects

Contribute to specific initiatives:

- **Site Inventory** - Help maintain and update site information
- **Research Projects** - Conduct market research or data analysis
- **Grant Writing** - Assist with grant applications
- **Community Outreach** - Represent OCEDC at community events

**Time Commitment:** Varies by project

## Volunteer Requirements

### Skills & Experience

We welcome volunteers with:

- Business experience and expertise
- Marketing and communications skills
- Event planning experience
- Research and analytical abilities
- Community connections and relationships
- Passion for economic development

### Time Commitment

Volunteer opportunities range from:

- **One-Time Events** - 2-4 hours for specific events
- **Short-Term Projects** - A few weeks or months
- **Ongoing Roles** - Regular monthly or quarterly commitments
- **Board Service** - Annual commitment with monthly meetings

### Background Check

Some volunteer roles may require a background check, particularly those involving direct business assistance or financial matters.

## How to Get Started

### Step 1: Express Interest

[Contact us](/contact) to express your interest in volunteering. Let us know:

- Your areas of interest and expertise
- Your availability and time commitment
- What you hope to gain from volunteering

### Step 2: Initial Meeting

We'll schedule a brief meeting to:

- Discuss volunteer opportunities
- Match your interests with available roles
- Answer your questions
- Determine the best fit

### Step 3: Orientation

New volunteers receive:

- OCEDC overview and mission
- Volunteer role description
- Training and resources
- Introduction to staff and other volunteers

### Step 4: Begin Volunteering

Start contributing to economic development in Ogle County!
