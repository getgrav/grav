# Starting a Business in Ogle County

Starting a business in Ogle County is straightforward with the right guidance. Our team is here to help you navigate the process every step of the way.

## Steps to Start Your Business

### 1. Business Planning

- Develop your business plan
- Identify your target market
- Secure financing
- Choose your business structure (LLC, Corporation, Partnership, Sole Proprietorship)

### 2. Business Registration

**State Level:**
- Register with Illinois Secretary of State
- Obtain Federal EIN (Employer Identification Number) from IRS
- Register for Illinois Business Tax (if applicable)

**Local Level:**
- Register business name with Ogle County Clerk
- Obtain local business license (if required by municipality)
- Register for local tax collection (if applicable)

### 3. Permits & Licenses

Depending on your business type, you may need:

- **Zoning Permit** - Verify your location is zoned for your business type
- **Building Permits** - For construction or renovations
- **Occupancy Permit** - Before opening to the public
- **Sign Permit** - For exterior signage
- **Health Department Permit** - For food service, healthcare, etc.
- **Professional Licenses** - For regulated professions

### 4. Tax Registration

- Register for Illinois Sales Tax (if selling goods/services)
- Register for Illinois Withholding Tax (if hiring employees)
- Register for local tax collection (if applicable)

### 5. Insurance Requirements

- General liability insurance
- Workers' compensation (if hiring employees)
- Professional liability (if applicable)
- Property insurance

## Required Permits & Licenses by Business Type

### Retail & Service Businesses

- Business license (municipal)
- Sales tax registration
- Sign permit
- Occupancy permit

### Food Service

- Health department permit
- Food service license
- Liquor license (if applicable)

### Manufacturing

- Zoning permit
- Building permits
- Environmental permits (if applicable)
- Fire department inspection

### Professional Services

- Professional license (state-issued)
- Business registration
- Professional liability insurance

## Resources & Contacts

### State Resources

- **Illinois Secretary of State** - Business registration
- **Illinois Department of Revenue** - Tax registration
- **Illinois Department of Commerce** - Business assistance

### Local Resources

- **Ogle County Clerk** - Business name registration
- **Municipal Offices** - Local permits and licenses
- **OCEDC** - Free business assistance and guidance

## Getting Help

The Ogle County Economic Development Corporation offers free assistance with:

- Understanding permit requirements
- Navigating the registration process
- Connecting with appropriate agencies
- Identifying available resources and incentives
