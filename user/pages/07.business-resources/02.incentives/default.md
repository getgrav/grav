# Tax Incentives & Financing Programs

Ogle County offers a variety of economic development incentives and financing programs to help businesses grow and succeed. Our team can help you identify and apply for programs that fit your needs.

## Illinois EDGE Tax Credit

The **Economic Development for a Growing Economy (EDGE)** program provides tax credits to businesses that:

- Create new jobs
- Make capital investments
- Locate or expand in Illinois

**Benefits:**
- Income tax credits based on job creation and investment
- Can be used to offset Illinois income tax liability
- Transferable credits available

**Eligibility:**
- Minimum job creation thresholds
- Minimum capital investment requirements
- Competitive application process

## Enterprise Zone Benefits

Properties located in designated Enterprise Zones qualify for:

### Sales Tax Exemptions
- Building materials
- Manufacturing equipment
- Computers and software

### Investment Tax Credits
- 0.5% credit on qualified investments
- Additional credits for job creation

### Utility Tax Exemptions
- Electricity
- Natural gas
- Telecommunications

### Property Tax Abatements
- Negotiable property tax incentives
- Long-term abatement agreements

## Tax Increment Financing (TIF)

TIF districts provide public infrastructure improvements to support private development:

- Road improvements
- Utility extensions
- Site preparation
- Public amenities

## SBA Loan Programs

### 7(a) Loan Program
- Up to $5 million
- Working capital, equipment, real estate
- Flexible terms and competitive rates

### 504 Loan Program
- Real estate and equipment financing
- Fixed-rate, long-term loans
- Lower down payments

### Microloan Program
- Up to $50,000
- Small business startup and expansion
- Technical assistance included

## Illinois Finance Authority Programs

- **Bond Financing** - Tax-exempt and taxable bonds
- **Loan Guarantees** - Credit enhancement
- **Direct Loans** - Gap financing
- **Tax Credits** - Various incentive programs

## Local Incentives

### Property Tax Abatements
- Negotiable abatements for qualifying projects
- Phased abatement schedules
- Job creation requirements

### Workforce Training Grants
- Customized training programs
- Reimbursement for training costs
- Partnership with educational institutions

### Infrastructure Assistance
- Utility extensions
- Road improvements
- Site preparation support
