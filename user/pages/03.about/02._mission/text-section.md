## Our Mission

To **promote and facilitate economic development** in Ogle County by attracting new businesses, supporting existing businesses, and creating an environment that encourages investment, job growth, and community prosperity.

---

## Our Vision

To position Ogle County as the **premier business destination in Northern Illinois**, recognized for our strategic location, skilled workforce, business-friendly environment, and exceptional quality of life.
