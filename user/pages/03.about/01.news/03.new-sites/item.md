# New Industrial Sites Added to Inventory

The Ogle County Economic Development Corporation has added several new industrial sites to its available property inventory, expanding options for businesses seeking locations in the region.

## New Sites Available

### Rochelle Industrial Park - Phase 2

- **150 Acres** - Fully serviced industrial park
- **Shovel-Ready** - All utilities and infrastructure in place
- **Enterprise Zone** - Tax incentives available
- **Rail Access** - Union Pacific rail service available
- **I-39 Access** - Direct highway access

### Oregon Business Center

- **75 Acres** - Mixed-use development site
- **Flexible Zoning** - Industrial and commercial uses
- **All Utilities** - Water, sewer, gas, electric, fiber
- **TIF District** - Public infrastructure support available

### Byron Commerce Park

- **50 Acres** - Commercial and light industrial
- **Highway Visibility** - Prime location on US Route 20
- **Ready for Development** - Permits and approvals in place

## Site Features

All new sites include:

- **Zoning Approval** - Pre-zoned for intended uses
- **Utility Infrastructure** - All utilities available
- **Environmental Assessments** - Phase I assessments completed
- **Transportation Access** - Highway and rail access
- **Incentive Eligibility** - Enterprise Zone and TIF benefits available
