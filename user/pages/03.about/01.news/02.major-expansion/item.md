# Major Manufacturing Expansion Announced in Rochelle

**Rochelle, IL** - A leading manufacturing company has announced plans for a significant expansion in Rochelle, creating 50 new jobs and investing $5 million in new facilities.

## Expansion Details

The expansion includes:

- **50 New Jobs** - Full-time positions with competitive wages and benefits
- **$5 Million Investment** - New manufacturing facility and equipment
- **20,000 Square Feet** - Additional production space
- **State-of-the-Art Equipment** - Modern manufacturing technology

## Why Rochelle?

Company leadership cited several factors in choosing Rochelle for expansion:

- **Strategic Location** - Direct access to I-39 and Union Pacific rail
- **Skilled Workforce** - Access to trained manufacturing workers
- **Business-Friendly Environment** - Supportive local government and EDC
- **Available Infrastructure** - Ready-to-develop site with utilities
- **Quality of Life** - Excellent schools and community amenities

## Economic Impact

This expansion represents:

- Increased local employment opportunities
- Additional tax revenue for the community
- Support for local suppliers and services
- Continued growth in the manufacturing sector

## OCEDC Support

The Ogle County Economic Development Corporation worked closely with the company throughout the expansion process, providing:

- Site selection assistance
- Incentive identification and application support
- Workforce development connections
- Permitting and regulatory guidance

## Looking Forward

"This expansion demonstrates the strength of Ogle County as a business location," said OCEDC Executive Director. "We're proud to support companies that invest in our community and create opportunities for our residents."

The expansion is expected to be completed by mid-2024, with hiring beginning in the spring.
