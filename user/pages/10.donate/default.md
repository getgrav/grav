# Donate to Ogle County Economic Development Corporation

Your donation helps support economic development initiatives that create jobs, attract businesses, and build a stronger future for Ogle County. Every contribution makes a difference.

## Why Donate?

Your donation directly supports:

- **Business Attraction** - Marketing efforts to bring new businesses to Ogle County
- **Business Retention** - Services to help existing businesses grow and expand
- **Site Development** - Preparation of industrial and commercial properties
- **Workforce Development** - Training programs and workforce connections
- **Community Advocacy** - Promoting policies that support economic growth

## How Your Donation Helps

### $50 - $99
Supports marketing materials and outreach efforts

### $100 - $249
Helps fund business assistance services and site selection activities

### $250 - $499
Contributes to workforce development programs and training initiatives

### $500 - $999
Supports site development and infrastructure preparation

### $1,000+
Major contribution that enables comprehensive economic development programs

Set up a monthly or quarterly recurring donation to provide ongoing support for economic development initiatives. Contact us to arrange a recurring donation plan.

## Donation Recognition

Donors who contribute $100 or more will be:

- Listed in our annual donor recognition (with permission)
- Acknowledged at OCEDC events
- Invited to special donor appreciation events
- Provided with updates on how their donation is being used

## Tax Deductibility

OCEDC is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation may be tax-deductible. Please consult with your tax advisor regarding the deductibility of your contribution.
